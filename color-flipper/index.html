Some interesting facts about color

  • All colors are the reproduction of our brain

    Brightness, contrast and color are all the work of our brain. Without it, our lives would be gray and probably not as interesting. What would our apartments be like, how brightly would the lamps burn and how would we perceive our surroundings if our brain did not create our tastes and preferences

  • Red color is seen differently by men and women

    American scientists have determined that the gene that is responsible for the red color is located in the X chromosome. As you know, men have only one, but women have a couple of them. So do not get angry at your friend if he is not very recognizes all the shades of red

  • Metallic is the safest color

    When choosing a car, a woman, of course, will dream of a bright car and the most diverse colors. A man tends to black, white, gray shades. But did you know that for your safety the best will be silver. This color is best seen in overcast weather and dusk. But you should not forget about other safety factors. This is just one of many factors that scientists in New Zealand have researched

  • The color pink protects against stress

    If you want to stay balanced and worry less, use pink wherever it is appropriate. From interiors to your closet, boldly combine it with other shades. In 2015, pink cool shade is on trend for the most luxurious interiors.

  • Blue is Earthlings' favorite color

    This is not a fiction, but the bare facts. It turns out that 40% of the total population - 17 countries of the world consider blue to be one of the most favorite colors. It is followed by purple, which was chosen by 14%. But the most disliked was named - white. Although white is considered the best color for dishes in restaurants. Only on a white plate any food looks unusually appetizing.

  • Cosmic coffee with milk

    This color is recognized by scientists as the color of our planet. It was obtained by averaging the colors of 200,000 galaxies. So it turned out that our earth has the color of coffee with milk.

  • Newton's color wheel is the best way to navigate colors

    It's great for mixing colors. Invented as early as Isaac Newton and supplemented by other experts, it is considered the basis of the traditional color wheel. The primary colors are red, yellow, and blue. Secondary colors are green, orange and violet. They are obtained by mixing primary colors. Secondary colors - which are obtained by mixing primary and secondary colors. This is how you get blue-green, yellow-orange, etc. How best to mix colors to get harmony shows this color wheel. Knowing it is the basis of drawing, decorating, designing and creating everything that pleases you
